Suggested Readings

Sometimes master and bachelor students contact me looking for introductory material to category theory and categorical logic. This webpage collets some material.

Category Theory

  • Basic Category Theory.

    by Tom Leinster. A very good introduction to the subject, especially for bachelor students.

  • Category theory in context.

    by Emily Rihel. A very good introduction to the subject, especially for bachelor students.

  • Categorical Foundations.

    By Pedicchio, Tholen et al. A very good introduction to the subject, especially for bachelor students.

Categorical Logic

  • The Category Theoretic Understanding of Universal Algebra: Lawvere Theories and Monads.

    By Martin Hyland and John Power.

Logic and foundational aspects of Type Theory

  • Open Logic Project.

    Open book.

  • Calcolo dei predicati.

    by Alessandro Berarducci. Italian.

  • I teoremi di incompletezza di Godel.

    by Alessandro Berarducci. Italian.

  • Intuitionistic type theory.

    by Per Martin-Löf.

  • Semantics for type theory.

Misc

  • The history of categorical logic 1963–1977.

    by Jean-Pierre Marquis and Gonzalo E. Reyes.

  • Indiscrete Thoughts.

    Giancarlo Rota.
