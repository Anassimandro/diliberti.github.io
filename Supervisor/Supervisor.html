Thesis supervision and Projects

I am happy to supervise bachelor theses and master theses in the topics below.

  • Categorical Logic and Logic.

  • Category theory and Formal Category Theory.

  • Categorical semantics of Type Theory.

  • Interactions between Logic and Geometry.

  • Dualities.

  • Categorical approaches to Geometry and Topology.

Yet, if you have some topic to suggest, feel free to contact me with any idea of yours, we will evaluate it together. If you feel a bit lost, or you simply don't have a topic in mind, you can stop by my office and tell me your interests. You can also browse a list of suggested readings that I prepared a while ago.

If your university allows it, I am open to supervise students that are not based where I am. To contact me, please send me an email. In order to make my life easier, make sure to include the word THSUP in the subject.
