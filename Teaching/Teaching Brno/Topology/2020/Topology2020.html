Lectures take place every Tuesday from 18 to 18.50 in M2.

Every lesson provides a list of exercises, students that intend to have credits from the course are expected to solve a certain amount of them, which depends on the specific exercise sheet. The exercises are a necessary and sufficient condition to be admitted to the oral exam, but do not provide credits per se. Solutions can be sent by email or brought brevi manu the week after.

If you send me an email about this course make sure to include the word MUNITOP20 in the subject. This makes my life easier.
