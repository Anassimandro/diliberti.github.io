Category Theory 20

Info. The course will be in place, at the Institute of Mathematics of the Czech Academy of Sciences in the blue lecture room every Tuesday from 15 to 17. Given the exceptionality of the year and in order to reach the broader audience possible, we will maintein a youtube channel.

Audience. The course is open to bachelor, master students and researchers that are not familiar with the topic. The audience is expected to have attended an introductory course in at least two of the following topics: general topology, algebraic topology, group theory, module theory, universal algebra, model theory.

  • 1. Toy category theory: Posets I

Syllabus. Toy Category Theory: posets. Categories, functors, natural transformations. Yoneda: Presheaves, representability, Yoneda embedding. Limits and Colimits. Adjunctions. Monads. Kan extentions. Towards enriched categories: monoidal (closed) categories and enrichments.

: Basic Category Theory by Tom Leinster. : Category Theory in Context by Emily Rihel. : Handbook of categorical algebra, Vol. I & II by Francis Borceux.
