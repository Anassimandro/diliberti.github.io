Info. The course will be at the Institute of Mathematics of the Czech Academy of Sciences in the blue lecture room every Tuesday from 15 to 17 o'clock.. For every lesson we will provide an exercise sheet.

Given the current COVID-situation, the course will completely be on Zoom. Click here to attend the lectures. The password is 728002. We might decide to maintain also a youtube channel.

The course will start in the 6th of October.

Audience. The course is open to bachelor, master students and researchers that are not familiar with the topic. The audience is expected to have attended an introductory course in at least two of the following topics: general topology, algebraic topology, group theory, module theory, universal algebra, model theory.

  • 1. Categories, functors, natural transformations.

    See Chap. 1 in Leinster.

Syllabus. Categories, functors, natural transformations. Limits and Colimits. Adjunctions. Toy Category Theory: posets. Yoneda: Presheaves, representability, Yoneda embedding. Monads. Kan extentions. Towards enriched categories: monoidal (closed) categories and enrichments.

: Basic Category Theory by Tom Leinster. : Category Theory in Context by Emily Rihel. : Handbook of categorical algebra, Vol. I & II by Francis Borceux. : Further readings.
