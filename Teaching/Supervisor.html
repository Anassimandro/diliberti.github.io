Thesis supervision and Projects

I am very happy to supervise or co-supervise bachelor and master theses in any of the following topics. Those should be intended in the broader possible sense.

  • Category theory.

  • Categorical Logic.

  • Formal Category Theory.

  • Logic.

  • Interactions between Logic and Geometry.

  • Dualities in Logic and Geometry.

  • Categorical approaches to Geometry and Topology.

If you believe that something is missing in this list, and you have some cool topic to propose, you are highly encouraged to do so! Feel free to contact me with any idea of yours, we will evaluate it together.

If you feel a bit lost, or you simply don't have a topic in mind, you can stop by my office and tell me your interests. You can also browse a list of suggested readings that I prepared a while ago.

If we are not physically in the same university, or for any reason you can't knock on my door, we can meet in my metaphysical office, reachable via the following door . If your university allows it, I am open to supervise students that are not based where I am.

To contact me, please send me an email. In order to make my life easier, make sure to include the word THSUP in the subject.
