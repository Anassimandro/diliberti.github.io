Bohemian Philosophical Workshop

The Institute of Mathematics of the Czech Academy of Science and the International Center for Formal Ontology happily announce the Bohemian Philosophical Workshop, a workshop in emerging topics in philosophy and logic. The workshop has a quite broad scope and is willing to encompass a variety of subjects, ranging from theoretical computer science to philosophy. Below you find a list of keywords and a labeling legenda (the order is merely alphabetical).

  • CS : type theory and theoretical computer science;

  • CT : category theory and categorical logic;

  • ML : set theory, model theory and proof theory;

  • PH : philosphy of language and logic.

The seminar will take place at the Institute of Mathematics, in Prague 14-18 September.
